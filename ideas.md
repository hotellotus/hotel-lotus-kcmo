# Hotel Lotus KCMO — Design Ideas

## Reference Analysis
- **Brand**: Hotel Lotus, Kansas City, MO — "Essence In Hospitality"
- **Logo**: Gold lotus flower icon in a circle, serif "Hotel Lotus" text, gold tagline
- **Color Palette**: Black background, gold (#C9A84C / #D0BA61), white text
- **Fonts**: Serif display (Gilda Display or similar), clean sans-serif body
- **Theme**: Classic luxury hotel — dark, gold-accented, sophisticated

## Idea 1 — Noir Luxury (Chosen)
**Design Movement**: Art Deco Noir — dark elegance with gold accents
**Core Principles**:
- Deep black/charcoal backgrounds with warm gold highlights
- Serif typography for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- Generous whitespace with structured asymmetric layouts
- Subtle gold dividers and ornamental elements

**Color Philosophy**: Black (#0A0A0A) base with gold (#C9A84C) as the primary accent, off-white (#F5F0E8) for body text. Evokes luxury, warmth, and timelessness.

**Layout Paradigm**: Full-width hero with centered booking widget, then alternating left/right content sections. Navigation is transparent over hero, becoming dark on scroll.

**Signature Elements**:
- Lotus flower SVG ornament as section dividers
- Gold horizontal rules and borders
- Subtle dark overlay on hero images

**Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth scroll transitions, hover gold underlines on nav, subtle image zoom on hover for gallery items

**Animation**: Fade-in on scroll for sections, gentle parallax on hero image

**Typography System**:
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, bold) — headings
- Body: Lato (sans-serif, 300/400) — paragraphs and labels
- Accent: Gilda Display — taglines and special callouts
